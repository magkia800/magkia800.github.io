Built for the start line

One starter. More useful sessions.

Train alone, work with a coach, or run a structured group session without changing tools.

01 / START

Realistic commands

Adjust pacing, starter language, voice, visual cue, pistol profile, electronic tone, or hand clap.

02 / REACT

Measure first movement

Use motion sensing for reaction feedback and false-start warnings, or begin hands-free with the camera stance sensor.

03 / TIME

Finish every rep

Start timing automatically at GO, stop at the line, and keep reactions, sprint times, notes, and session history together.

Use it your way

Solo starts and coach timing.

Athlete setup

Place or secure the phone near the blocks and react to sound, screen flash, torch, camera, or movement sensing.

Coach setup

Route start audio to a nearby speaker, start the stopwatch at GO, and stop the rep when the athlete crosses.

Private by design

Your training stays on your device.

RaceCue does not require an account and currently uses no advertising or analytics SDK. Camera frames, recordings, motion readings, and training history are handled locally.
