Privacy Policy

RaceCue is designed to keep training data on your device. The app does not require an account and does not use advertising or analytics SDKs.

Information stored on your device

Your settings, custom starter recordings, reaction results, stopwatch preferences, and session history are stored locally on your device. They are not uploaded to a RaceCue server. Removing the app or clearing its storage may permanently delete this information.

Camera and torch

Camera access is optional and is used for the Camera Stance Sensor and, on supported phones, the rear-camera torch GO cue. Camera frames are processed live on the device. RaceCue does not capture photographs, save video, use face recognition, or upload camera content.

Microphone

Microphone access is optional and is used only when you record custom starter commands or sounds. Recordings remain in the app's local storage on your device and can be deleted from the recording screen.

Motion sensors

Motion-sensor access is optional and is used to detect movement around the start signal for training feedback. RaceCue does not use GPS location. Raw motion readings are processed in real time and are not uploaded.

Purchases

When in-app purchasing is enabled, payments are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. RaceCue does not receive your card or bank details. The store and purchase service may provide a transaction identifier and entitlement status so the app can unlock and restore Premium access.

Sharing and tracking

RaceCue does not sell personal information, track you across other apps or websites, or share training data with advertisers. The app currently has no user account, cloud sync, advertising network, or developer-operated analytics service.

Children

RaceCue does not knowingly collect personal information from children. A parent, guardian, coach, or club remains responsible for supervising device, camera, microphone, and training use by minors.

Your choices

You can decline camera, microphone, or motion permission and continue using features that do not need that permission. You can remove recordings and session history inside the app, or remove all locally stored information by deleting the app and its data.

Policy changes and contact

This policy may be updated when RaceCue adds services such as purchases or optional cloud features. The current version and date will remain available on this page. Questions can be submitted through the RaceCue support page.
