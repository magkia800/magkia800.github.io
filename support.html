Support

Quick answers for start audio, sensors, recordings, timing, and Premium access.

I cannot hear the starter commands

Turn up media volume, check silent or focus modes, and tap Unlock Audio once if it appears. When using a wireless speaker, connect it before opening the training screen and test the complete start sequence at close range.

The camera or torch is unavailable

Allow Camera permission in your phone settings. The stance camera and torch cue share the rear camera and cannot run at the same time. Torch control also depends on the phone and browser engine; some devices do not expose it to apps.

The movement sensor does not react

Open Sensor, keep the phone secured to the athlete or equipment, then enable and calibrate while still. Grant Motion permission when requested. Try High sensitivity if movement is not detected, or Low sensitivity if normal vibration triggers it too easily.

Custom recording is blocked

Allow Microphone permission in phone settings and try again. RaceCue automatically limits command length so the Set recording cannot overlap the GO signal.

Bluetooth timing looks different

Wireless speaker delay and range vary with the phone, speaker, distance, obstacles, and interference. Test the setup at the track. RaceCue measurements are intended for repeatable training feedback, not certified competition results.

How do I restore Premium?

Use Restore Purchases from the Premium screen while signed in to the same Apple or Google account used for the original purchase. A permanent unlock should not require another payment.

How do I remove my data?

Delete custom recordings from Voice Recorder and clear session history from Sessions. Removing the app and its data clears the remaining local settings and results.

Report a problem

Include your phone model, operating-system version, which feature you were using, and what happened. Do not include private recordings or payment details.
